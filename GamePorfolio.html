About Me

Hey there! I'm an iOS Developer with 1 year of experience creating smooth and user-friendly mobile apps. I love working with Swift and making apps that people actually enjoy using. Right now, I'm learning even more at the Apple Developer Academy.


While I build professional iOS apps during the day, I have a huge passion for game development too! I enjoy creating fun gaming experiences in my free time. Whether it's optimizing app performance or designing exciting game mechanics, I believe great software should be both functional and entertaining.


When I'm not coding apps or working on game projects, you'll find me exploring new technologies, learning about game design, or sharing what I know with other developers. Let's build something amazing together!

Excapp

Excapp is a mobile app that lets people try a small version of the Escapp escape room game, based on the popular Netflix movie by Infinite Studios. It's made for those who didn't get a ticket or want to experience a preview before trying the real escape room. The app includes a story-based puzzle game that feels like the real thing, giving users a fun and spooky way to enjoy the world of Losmen Melati.
